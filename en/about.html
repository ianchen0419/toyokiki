President's Message

Toyokiki Industrial Co., Ltd, was established in 1951 as a machining company, and started operation at Yaita factory in 1963. Since the start, we have produced components for construction machinery, and done machining and sheet metal processing for forklift components. Our advantage is in manufacturing large variety of products. In recent years, the demand from developing countries has grown rapidly. We have done a large investment for facilities and increased the production. To provide the best solution in this fast changing business environment, we have built global network for raw material purchasing and improved the quality of products.
We maintain our committment to the challenge of making the world a better place through iron processing. We ask for your continued cooperation and good faith in a quest that is sure to benefit us all.

Motohiro Ishii
Chairman and President
